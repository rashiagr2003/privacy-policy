Privacy Policy – FitFlow

Last Updated: January 2025

FitFlow ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal fitness tracking application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

1. About FitFlow

FitFlow helps users track workouts, meals, water intake, and overall fitness progress.

2. Information We Collect

  • Name and email address
  • Weight, height, age and gender
  • Workout and activity history
  • Meal logs and calorie intake
  • Water intake records
  • Fitness goals and tasks
  • Notification preferences

We do not collect location data, contacts, or unrelated information.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Calculate BMI and health metrics
  • Track fitness progress
  • Provide personalized recommendations
  • Send reminders and notifications
  • Restore data when logging into new devices

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is securely stored using Firebase (Google Cloud). All data transfers use encrypted HTTPS connections.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties. Services used include:

  • Firebase / Google Cloud
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging

6. Children's Privacy

FitFlow is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

You can delete your account anytime from the app settings. Deleting your account permanently removes all data.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page.
